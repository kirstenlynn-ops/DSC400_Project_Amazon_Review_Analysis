Key Insights from the Dataset

  • Customer ratings are heavily skewed toward higher star values.
  • Verified purchases make up the majority of reviews.
  • Average sentiment polarity remains consistently positive.
  • A small group of reviewers contributes disproportionately to total reviews.
  • Review activity increases significantly after 2004.
