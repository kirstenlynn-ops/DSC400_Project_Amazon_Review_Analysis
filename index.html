Project Overview

This project analyzes 129,794 Amazon reviews across 58,902 products to uncover patterns in review sentiment, product ratings, and reviewer behavior. Machine learning models and transformer-based NLP were applied to predict review ratings and extract actionable insights.

Key Insights & Research Questions
  • Positive sentiment correlates strongly with higher star ratings.
  • Low-star reviews tend to be longer and more detailed.
  • Verified purchases slightly influence rating patterns.
  • Hierarchical models improved mid-range rating prediction.
